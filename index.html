Our Advantage

What Makes EonDev Different

Unlike ordinary meeting tools, EonDev blends transcription, OCR, and
AI-driven insight into one seamless Windows overlay experience.

Real-time AI Suggestions

An overlay app that listens to meetings and provides real-time AI suggestions without joining as a bot.

Transcription & Note-Taking

Transcribe audio to notes, edit them, and export them. Notes are saved on the server by default.

Stealth Mode

The app doesn't appear in the recent apps tab, screen captures, or screen sharing, making it invisible to others in a meeting.

OCR & Contextual Analysis

Capture screen text with OCR and get contextual AI insights through follow-up questions.

Chat with Transcript

After a meeting, open the transcript in a chat to ask follow-up questions and get contextual answers.

Theme Editing

Customize the app's theme for better visibility.
